# End of Line

A minimalist static website that visualizes your life expectancy as a countdown timer and interactive matrix. Make every week count.

## Features

- **Current Age Display**: Shows your age in years, months, and days
- **Life Progress in Weeks**: Displays weeks lived, total weeks, and weeks remaining with a progress bar
- **Days Remaining**: Shows the number of days left until your expected life expectancy
- **Live Countdown**: Real-time countdown showing hours, minutes, and seconds remaining
- **Life in Weeks Matrix**: Visual representation of your entire life where each square represents one week
- **Customizable Settings**: Update your date of birth and life expectancy
- **Share Functionality**: Generate and download an Instagram story-style vertical image of your stats
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices
- **Light Theme**: Clean, modern interface with smooth animations

## Getting Started

1. Clone this repository
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. No build process or dependencies required!

## Default Settings

- **Default Birth Date**: December 11, 1995
- **Default Life Expectancy**: 80 years

## Usage

### Viewing Your Stats

Simply open the page and it will display all your life statistics based on the default or saved settings.

### Updating Your Information

1. Scroll to the "Update Your Information" section
2. Enter your day, month, and year of birth
3. Set your expected life expectancy
4. Click "Update" to save and refresh all statistics

Your settings are automatically saved to your browser's local storage.

### Sharing Your Stats

1. Scroll to the "Share Your Stats" section
2. Click "Generate Story Image"
3. An Instagram story-sized image (1080x1920) will be downloaded automatically
4. Upload this image to your Instagram story or share it on other social media

## Technology Stack

- Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- No frameworks or dependencies
- Responsive design with CSS Grid and Flexbox
- Canvas API for generating shareable images
- Local Storage API for persisting user settings
